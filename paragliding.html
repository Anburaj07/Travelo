India is a popular destination for paragliding due to its diverse landscapes. Some of the best paragliding spots in India include the Daan Kurhi camp in Ranikhet, Uttarakhand, which is known to be one of the safest sites for paragliding.

Himachal Pradesh is another popular destination for paragliding, with multiple options such as Devidhar, Parvati Valley, Rohtang, and Bakhli . The cost of paragliding in Himachal Pradesh starts from INR 2,500 .
